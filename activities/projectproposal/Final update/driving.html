Driving

Driving has been a passion since I took my first long road trip. Below are short stories from memorable drives, plus my basic safe-driving tips.

Reference: Drives

Memorable Drives

Coastal Loop: A morning drive along the coast where I stopped for photography and coffee. The drive taught me to plan stops and respect local speed limits.

Night Skyline Run: A safe night drive I planned with friends — we kept speeds moderate and used proper lighting and visibility checks.

Safe Driving Tips

  • Always check tires and fluids before a long trip.
  • Keep a small toolkit and first-aid kit.
  • Plan breaks every 90 minutes to stay alert.
