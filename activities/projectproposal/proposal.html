1. Title: Venu’s Passion Hub

Topic: A personal website showcasing my passions: photography, driving cars, long bike rides, and table tennis.

Why I Chose This Topic: These hobbies are a big part of my life. Photography helps me capture memories, driving and riding give me joy and relaxation, and table tennis keeps me active. This website will share my experiences, photos, and stories using all my own original content.
