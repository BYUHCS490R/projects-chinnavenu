Yarada Beach is a beautiful and less crowded beach located about 15 kilometers from the city center of Vizag. It’s known for its pristine sands, clear blue waters, and scenic surroundings. The beach is nestled between hills, providing a picturesque backdrop for visitors. Yarada Beach is a great place for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of the city and enjoy some peace and tranquility. The beach is relatively untouched, making it a perfect spot for nature lovers and photographers. There are a few small eateries nearby where you can enjoy local snacks and refreshments. If you’re visiting Vizag, don’t miss the chance to explore the serene beauty of Yarada Beach.
