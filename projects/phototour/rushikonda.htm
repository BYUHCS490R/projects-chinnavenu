Rushikonda Beach is a beautiful and serene beach located about 8 kilometers from the city center of Vizag. It’s known for its golden sands, clear blue waters, and scenic surroundings. The beach is a popular spot for water sports enthusiasts, offering activities like jet skiing, windsurfing, and snorkeling. Rushikonda is also a great place for swimming and sunbathing, with its relatively calm waves and clean environment. The beach is surrounded by lush greenery and hills, providing a picturesque backdrop for visitors. There are several resorts and eateries nearby where you can enjoy local cuisine and refreshments. If you’re looking for a peaceful getaway from the hustle and bustle of the city, Rushikonda Beach is definitely worth a visit.
