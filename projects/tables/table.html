Mission Experience

These are some of the most memorable days of my life, and choosing to serve God is one of the best decisions I have ever made. It has been a great blessing for me and my family. Personally, I have learned many life lessons and gained wisdom through experiences—even without going through some of them myself.

My mission parents have been a beautiful example to me. They taught me many things about love, service, and how to take care of my future family. My companions were also a big blessing—living and serving with people from different cultures and habits gave me so many unforgettable experiences.

Helping people come unto Christ was the best part of my mission. Sometimes I feel so proud to see them growing strong in the Church and setting an example for me. I miss all of them deeply, but I hope to see them again someday and relive those cherished moments together.
