OG (Original Gangster) is an action-packed Telugu film starring Power Star Pawan Kalyan in a dynamic role. Directed by Sujeeth, the movie presents a bold story of a man who once ruled the underworld and returns after years of silence. His comeback shakes the city as old enemies and new rivals rise against him.

The film mixes intense action with strong emotions and stylish visuals. Pawan Kalyan’s calm but powerful attitude gives the movie a unique presence that fans love. With music by Thaman S and cinematography that captures both beauty and danger, OG becomes more than just a gangster movie — it’s a story about power, loyalty, and legacy.
